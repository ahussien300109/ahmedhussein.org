Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What do I need to start the CCNA course?
No prior networking knowledge is required. You'll need a computer with internet access and the free Cisco Packet Tracer software — everything else is provided.
How long does it take to pass the CCNA exam?
Most students complete the course and pass on their first attempt within 3–6 months with consistent study. Our structured labs and practice exams are built for exam efficiency.
Are courses aligned with official Cisco exams?
Yes. All courses follow official Cisco NetAcad blueprints and are updated to match current exam objectives. You'll learn exactly what's tested on the real exam.
Is there live support or mentorship available?
Yes. All enrolled students have direct access to Ahmed Hussein for Q&A throughout their course. Live sessions and session recordings are included.
Do I receive a certificate after completing a course?
Premium members receive a signed completion certificate for every course finished. Free members have full access to the CCNA Exam Bootcamp and selected course content.
Can I access courses on mobile?
Yes. The platform is fully responsive and works on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. Study anywhere, at your own pace.
